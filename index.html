Phoenix International Academy is one of the leading International Schools in Mysore, Karnataka, and South India. We use the British Edexcel curriculum and provide high quality education and teaching to students from Pomegranate 1/Pre KG to A Level/ Grade 12. We are an affiliated exam centre for International GCSE exams and AS and A Level Exams.

 

Phoenix International Academy inspires children to become global citizens through an inquiry-based learning environment. In a unique Oasis of learning we accompany and support all children entrusted to us on their way to becoming independent, self-confident & well balanced.
