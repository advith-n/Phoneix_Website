Phoenix International Academy is a highly progressive and enterprising international school in the heritage city Mysuru. Here we emphasize on concept-based teaching and learning, as opposed to content and rote learning. We focus on processes and outcomes – what a student should know and be able to do at any given age – as opposed to emphasizing the acquisition of specific knowledge.

Key principles run through the entirety of PIA’s education system such as:

■ The idea that each student is unique

■ An emphasis on developing positive relationships (with teachers, parents and peers).

■ Focus on enabling environments, with an awareness that learning does happen everywhere not just in the classroom.

We emphasize the development of the student as a whole, and particularly at the primary level, there is a great deal of scope for creativity and individuality in the classroom. Choosing an international school like ours not only gives students the benefit of a concept based education, but the qualifications gained and knowledge acquired are highly transferable. The structure and consistency of the national curriculum allows students to easily move, if necessary to schools in various countries and within the country. The system also facilitates an easy progression to any universities across the globe.

At Phoenix, we provide a nurturing, stimulating, and student-centred learning environment. We want to ensure that students are confident in themselves, with others and in their work, and are prepared for transitions into new environments. We foster a love of learning that will prepare them for the challenges of an ever-changing world.

About Edexcel

 

Edexcel education has achieved a worldwide reputation for qualityaround the world. International GCSE’s are recognized as a reliable record of attainment which counts towards entry to universities and colleges in India as well as abroad. It is also renowned for developing vital educational skills, including recall of knowledge, oral skills, problem solving, initiative team work and investigative skills. This world class curriculum is ‘student friendly’ and helps the students to ‘think out of the box’ to help them fit in to the 21st century learning skills.

 

Edexcel International GCSE offers a flexible course of study that gives candidates the freedom to choose subjects that are right for them, while providing them with a broad knowledge base and lifelong skills which provides a foundation for higher level courses such as the Advanced International Certificate of Education, the North American Advanced Placement program and International Baccalaureate and Intermediate Education in State and Central Schools.
