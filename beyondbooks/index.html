Karate
Karate is a form of exercise designed to strengthen the body and mind. It is imparted in a structured environment, where the students practice and demonstrate the ability to master a certain number of exercises before they can earn a belt and move to the next level of karate training. Here at PIA, we offer this activity for young children to improve physical fitness, mind-body coordination, self-discipline, and self-defense.
Swimming is undoubtedly the best form of exercise for all age groups that keeps them emotionally and physically healthy. Student life is the best phase of a person’s life cycle when they mold up their character and other routines. It is the age when one can learn a new skill easily without much effort. So why not consider learning swimming right from school life than keeping it aside for later? Yes, swimming can be a great addition for student life and here at PIA, we make it a part of the school activity we offer.

SAPIENT GLOBAL EDUCATIONAL TRUST

We offer a holistic music education to students.

Our Course Curriculum includes:

* Instrument Specialisation (which includes piano, guitar, keyboards, )

* Vocals (basics)

* Theory of Music

* Based on the interest and inclination of the students we also prepare students for examination board Trinity College, London.

What we are offering:

* Well-trained and qualified music teachers are provided.

* All infrastructure including keyboards, guitars, etc. are provided by the school. Each child has access to his own individual instrument during class.

* The progress of each child is tracked and assessed; and the same is communicated to the school management and parents on a periodic basis

* We provide children with the opportunity to perform via annual recitals

* We organise live performances by established artistes to give exposure and motivation to the students.

* We provide regular updates to the parent community on music & education.
